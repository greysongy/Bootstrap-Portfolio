ABOUT ME:

I’m a Computer Science-Economics major at Brown University who now codes as obsessively as I once kicked a soccer ball. I’m eager to explore mathematical industries that incorporate new technology, and to work through problems with creativity and nuance. Whether that means designing and implementing entirely original algorithms, using statistical procedures to analyze new data sets, or probabilistically managing risk in a portfolio, I’m ready to dive into the challenge. I’m also ready to find out just how much I have left to learn.
